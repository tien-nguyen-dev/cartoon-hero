Diane Nguyen

About

Diane Nguyen is a fictional character from the Netflix animated television series Bojack Horseman. Hired to ghostwrite BoJack’s memoir in the show’s pilot, uptight, judgmental, dependable Diane stands in stark contrast with the circus around her. On a show full of nuanced weirdos, she’s practically a stock character, except for one clumsily executed fact: She’s Asian American.
